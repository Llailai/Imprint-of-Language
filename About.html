PROJECT

"Imprint of Language" is a design project that is deeply rooted in the author's personal experiences and emotional journey during overseas study amid the global COVID-19 pandemic. The project aims to explore the role of intelligent machines in the context of a new language system and its impact on emotional communication between individuals after undergoing artificial active operation.


Through research and exploration of this "new language system," the author seeks to understand how it affects their emotional communication and information transmission within these important relationships. By doing so, the project aims to open a real conversation box in a romantic and abstract manner, allowing for the sharing and passing on of stories and experiences.

The project seeks to delve into the potential effects of language under by intelligent machines, on the way people communicate and connect emotionally. The focus on emotions and personal experiences in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic could make the project both relevant and relatable to a broader audience. The abstract and creative approach suggests that the author aims to evoke feelings and reflections through the art project.


"Imprint of Language" intertwines personal experiences, technology, emotions, and storytelling to create a unique and engaging exploration of the evolving nature of human communication.
